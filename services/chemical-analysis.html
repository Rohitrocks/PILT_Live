Chemical Analysis Services

Our comprehensive chemical analysis services utilize state-of-the-art analytical instrumentation and proven methodologies to provide accurate identification, quantification, and characterization of chemical compounds across diverse sample types.

Service Overview

PRIYAN INTERNATIONAL LAB AND TECHNOLOGY's chemical analysis division offers a complete range of analytical services designed to meet the demanding requirements of modern industries. Our laboratory is equipped with advanced instrumentation including ICP-MS, GC-MS, LC-MS, FTIR, XRD, and other cutting-edge analytical technologies.

Our experienced analytical chemists work closely with clients to develop customized testing protocols that address specific analytical challenges while ensuring regulatory compliance and data quality objectives are met.

Elemental Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of metallic and non-metallic elements using ICP-MS, ICP-OES, and XRF techniques.

  • Trace metal analysis (ppb-ppm levels)
  • Major element quantification
  • Multi-element screening
  • Isotopic analysis

Organic Analysis

Identification and quantification of organic compounds using GC-MS, LC-MS, and NMR spectroscopy.

  • Volatile organic compounds (VOCs)
  • Semi-volatile organics (SVOCs)
  • Pesticide residue analysis
  • Pharmaceutical compounds

Structural Analysis

Molecular structure determination using spectroscopic and crystallographic techniques.

  • FTIR spectroscopy
  • NMR spectroscopy
  • X-ray diffraction (XRD)
  • Mass spectrometry

Solution Chemistry

Analysis of aqueous solutions, acids, bases, and complex chemical mixtures.

  • pH and conductivity
  • Ion chromatography
  • Wet chemistry methods
  • Titrimetric analysis

Quantitative Analysis

Precise quantification of chemical species with validated analytical methods.

  • Method validation
  • Uncertainty assessment
  • Quality control
  • Statistical analysis

Unknown Identification

Comprehensive identification of unknown compounds and contaminants.

  • Library searching
  • De novo structure elucidation
  • Impurity profiling
  • Failure analysis

Advanced Instrumentation

ICP-MS/OES

Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry and Optical Emission Spectrometry for trace elemental analysis.

GC-MS/LC-MS

Gas and Liquid Chromatography Mass Spectrometry for organic compound analysis and identification.

FTIR/NMR

Fourier Transform Infrared and Nuclear Magnetic Resonance spectroscopy for structural analysis.

XRD/XRF

X-ray Diffraction and Fluorescence for crystalline structure and elemental composition analysis.

Industry Applications

Our chemical analysis services support a wide range of industries and applications:

Manufacturing

Raw material verification, product quality control, contamination analysis, and process monitoring.

Environmental

Water quality assessment, soil contamination studies, air pollution monitoring, and waste characterization.

Pharmaceutical

Drug substance analysis, impurity profiling, stability studies, and method development.

Food & Beverage

Nutritional analysis, contaminant detection, authenticity testing, and additive quantification.
