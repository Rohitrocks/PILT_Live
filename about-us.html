About - PILT

Our Mission

PRIYAN INTERNATIONAL LAB AND TECHNOLOGY is a leading provider of comprehensive analytical testing services, dedicated to delivering precise, reliable, and timely results that enable our clients to make informed decisions. With ISO/IEC 17025 accreditation and state-of-the-art facilities, we serve industries ranging from environmental monitoring to pharmaceutical development, ensuring the highest standards of analytical quality and regulatory compliance.

Our mission is to be the trusted analytical partner for organizations worldwide, providing exceptional laboratory services that support innovation, protect public health, and ensure environmental sustainability. We are committed to excellence in every analysis, backed by cutting-edge technology and the expertise of our highly qualified scientific team.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the premier analytical testing laboratory, setting industry standards for quality, innovation, and customer service while contributing to scientific advancement and regulatory compliance across all sectors we serve.

Core Values

Precision

We maintain the highest standards of analytical accuracy and precision in every test we perform.

Integrity

We operate with complete transparency, ethical practices, and unwavering commitment to data integrity.

Partnership

Our Expertise

With over two decades of experience in analytical testing, PRIYAN INTERNATIONAL LAB AND TECHNOLOGY has developed comprehensive expertise across multiple disciplines. Our laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art instrumentation and staffed by highly qualified scientists who bring deep knowledge and practical experience to every project.

Analytical Chemistry

Advanced analytical techniques including ICP-MS, GC-MS, LC-MS, FTIR, and XRD for comprehensive chemical characterization.

Environmental Science

Environmental monitoring and assessment services covering air, water, soil, and waste analysis for regulatory compliance.

Microbiological Testing

Comprehensive microbial analysis for pathogen detection, contamination assessment, and sterility testing.

Pharmaceutical Analysis

Drug substance and product testing, stability studies, and method development for pharmaceutical applications.
