Proficiency Testing Services

Our Proficiency Testing (PT) programs provide inter-laboratory comparison studies that enable laboratories to evaluate their analytical performance, identify areas for improvement, and demonstrate competency to regulatory bodies and accreditation organizations.

Chemical Analysis PT

Inter-laboratory comparison for chemical testing methods including trace metals, organic compounds, and environmental contaminants.

  • Trace Metal Analysis
  • Organic Pollutants
  • Water Quality Parameters
  • Soil Contamination
  • Statistical Analysis
Quarterly

Environmental Testing PT

Comprehensive proficiency testing for environmental laboratories covering air, water, soil, and waste analysis.

  • Air Quality Monitoring
  • Water Pollution Assessment
  • Soil Contamination Testing
  • Waste Characterization
  • Method Validation
Bi-annual

Food Safety PT

Proficiency testing programs for food testing laboratories focusing on safety parameters, nutritional content, and contaminants.

  • Microbiological Testing
  • Chemical Residues
  • Nutritional Analysis
  • Allergen Detection
  • Shelf-life Studies
Monthly

Pharmaceutical PT

Specialized proficiency testing for pharmaceutical laboratories covering drug substance analysis, impurity testing, and stability studies.

  • Active Ingredient Analysis
  • Impurity Profiling
  • Dissolution Testing
  • Stability Assessment
  • Bioanalytical Methods
Quarterly

Benefits of Our PT Programs

Quality Assurance

Validate your laboratory's analytical performance and identify potential issues before they impact your results.

Accreditation Support

Meet ISO/IEC 17025 requirements for proficiency testing participation and maintain your laboratory accreditation.

Performance Monitoring

Track your laboratory's performance over time and benchmark against peer laboratories worldwide.

Staff Training

Use PT results as training tools to improve analyst skills and laboratory procedures.

Our PT Process

1

Sample Preparation

Carefully prepared and characterized samples are distributed to participating laboratories.

2

Analysis Period

Laboratories analyze samples using their standard procedures and report results within the specified timeframe.

3

Statistical Analysis

Results are statistically analyzed to determine assigned values and performance criteria.

4

Report Distribution

Comprehensive reports with performance evaluation and recommendations are provided to participants.
